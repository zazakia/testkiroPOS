# Product Overview

InventoryPro is a comprehensive inventory management and Point of Sale (POS) system designed for soft drinks wholesale delivery companies in the Philippines.

## Core Purpose

The system provides real-time inventory tracking, supplier management, order fulfillment, and integrated POS capabilities with Philippine Peso (â‚±) as the default currency.

## Key Features

- **Multi-UOM Product Management**: Products can be sold in different units (bottles, packs, cartons) with independent pricing per unit
- **Batch Tracking with Average Costing**: Inventory tracked by batches with expiration dates using weighted average cost valuation
- **Integrated POS**: Point-of-sale transactions with multiple payment methods including GCash
- **Multi-Branch Support**: Manage multiple business locations with separate transaction recording
- **Financial Management**: Accounts Receivable, Accounts Payable, and expense tracking
- **Warehouse Management**: Capacity monitoring and utilization tracking
- **Alert System**: Automated alerts for low stock and expiring items
- **Comprehensive Reporting**: Financial statements, inventory reports, and sales analytics

## Target Users

- Operations Managers
- Warehouse Managers
- Procurement Managers
- Sales Representatives
- POS Operators
